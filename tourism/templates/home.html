Explore Karnataka's Majestic Cultural Heritage

History of Karnataka

Karnataka has a rich history dating back to prehistoric times. It has been home to powerful dynasties such as the Chalukyas, Hoysalas, and the Vijayanagara Empire. The state boasts numerous historical sites, including Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and the famous Mysore Palace.


Karnataka's Cuisine

Karnataka offers a diverse range of culinary delights, from the famous Bisi Bele Bath to Mangalorean seafood and Mysore Pak. Each region of Karnataka has its own distinct flavors and special dishes.


Art & Craft

The state is renowned for its traditional art forms like Yakshagana, Dollu Kunitha, and Mysore painting. Karnataka is also famous for its exquisite silk sarees and sandalwood carvings.

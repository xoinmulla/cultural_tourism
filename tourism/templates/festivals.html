Festivals

Explore the vibrant and diverse festivals celebrated across India.

Diwali

The festival of lights, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness.

Holi

The festival of colors, celebrating love, unity, and the arrival of spring.

Navratri

A nine-night festival dedicated to Goddess Durga, filled with dance and devotion.

Eid

A festival of prayers, feasting, and gratitude marking the end of Ramadan.

Pongal

A Tamil harvest festival celebrating nature and prosperity.

Durga Puja

A grand festival in Bengal honoring Goddess Durga with celebrations and rituals.
